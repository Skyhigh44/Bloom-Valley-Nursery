History of Gardening

Gardening dates back thousands of years and is one of the oldest human activities.

From ancient civilizations growing crops for sustenance to modern-day gardening as a hobby and art form, the love for plants and nature has shaped cultures around the world.

As we become more aware of environmental issues, gardening has also evolved into a means of promoting sustainability and biodiversity.
